Web 2.0: The Social Web

Web 2.0 is the "read-write" web. It transitioned from static pages to dynamic, user-generated content and the growth of social media. It focuses on how people use the internet as a platform for collaboration.

Key Characteristics
  • Interactivity through comments, likes, and shares.
  • Social networking sites (Facebook, YouTube, Twitter/X).
  • The rise of web applications and cloud computing.
